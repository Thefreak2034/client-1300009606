Our Services

Professional Garden Maintenance Services

Garden Maintenance

Reliable, scheduled garden maintenance for homes and businesses, mowing, hedging, weeding, and tidy-ups on a regular or one-off basis, keeping your garden looking its best.

Lawn Mowing & Edging

Regular mowing, edging, and trimming that keeps your lawn neat, healthy, and looking its best all year round.

Hedge & Shrub Trimming

Shaping, trimming, and tidying of hedges, shrubs, and screening plants for clean lines and healthy growth.

Landscaping & Garden Design

Full garden makeovers and landscaping, from design and plant selection to soft-landscaping that transforms your outdoor space.

Weeding & Garden Bed Care

Thorough weeding and garden bed maintenance that keeps beds clean, healthy, and free of invasive growth.

Mulching

Fresh mulch and soil conditioning spread through garden beds to lock in moisture, suppress weeds, and keep plants healthy.

Turf & Instant Lawn Laying

Ground preparation and laying of quality instant turf for a lush, even lawn that is ready to enjoy fast.

Garden Clean-ups & Green Waste Removal

One-off garden clean-ups and removal of green waste, prunings, and clippings, leaving your yard clean and clutter-free.

Pruning & Small Tree Care

Careful pruning and shaping of small trees, shrubs, and ornamentals to keep them healthy, safe, and well-formed.
